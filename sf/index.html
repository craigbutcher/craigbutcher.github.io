Our Mission

Foundation Sentinel was founded with one goal in mind – to end underage children being exploited around the world. While this cause isn’t new, our method to achieve it is. We have the personnel, technology, the law enforcement support, and more importantly the perseverance to see it through. The Foundation Sentinel focuses on targeting elusive child traffickers that use highly skilled methods to find and exploit their victims.

The Approach

Foundation Sentinel combines Special Operations targeting with emerging technology to target those that wish harm upon these children. The Foundation Sentinel will bring the fight directly to those that target and exploit minors while training and equipping 3rd world nations to do the same. The Foundation Sentinel is a pioneer in developing technology to assist Law Enforcement in identifying traffickers digitally.
